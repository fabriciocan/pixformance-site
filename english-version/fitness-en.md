## Section 3: Real-Time Correction

### H2:
**Real-time correction**

### Intro text:
The Pixformance station and fitness software provides continuous guidance on the accuracy, tempo, and precision of an exercise's execution and thus offers highly safe functional training – like with a personal trainer.

### Feature 1: Simple exercise instructions
The trainee positions him/herself in front of the Pixformance station and the exercise is demonstrated on-screen by a trainer.

### Feature 2: Precise motion analysis
During the exercise, a camera with a built-in depth sensor scans 25 joint points of the trainee within seconds and analyses their movements.

### Feature 3: Feedback in real time
The virtual trainer gives feedback in real time. This ensures that the exercises are carried out correctly and safely just like under the guidance of a trainer.

## Section 8: Training Concepts

### H2:
**Training Concepts**

### Intro text:
We offer solutions to various challenges that fitness studios face. Together with you, we work out a concept that reflects your requirements and is precisely tailored to your needs.

---

### 01 INDIVIDUAL TRAINING

#### Image:
`https://www.pixformance.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Pixformance-Station-Einzeltraining-Digitales-Sportgeraet-voll-vernetzt-Personal-Trainer.jpg`

#### H2:
**01 INDIVIDUAL TRAINING**

#### Body text:
With a Pixformance station, trainees can take advantage of tailor-made personal functional training at any time.

#### TRAINING CONCEPTS (expandable/accordion):
As 1:1 training in front of the station or as a supplement to an existing training concept

**SUPPLEMENTARY:** The Pixformance station, where functional exercises can be performed, can be added to an existing circle of devices.

**INTENSIVE:** The trainee receives customized Pixformance training from the trainer via the online platform. The trainer can track and optimize the training process on the online platform at any time.

**BETWEEN:** The Pixformance station is set up with a pre-installed training program, which can offer additional training outside the supervised training times.

#### DETAILS (expandable/accordion):
- **Required space:** Approx. 65 sq ft
- **Weight:** 143 lbs
- **Camera:** 2D Full HD
- **Computer and screen:** Can be switched on and off separately
- **Touch-Screen:** 49 inch touch display, standard exercises and training programs directly selectable via touch
- **Training distance:** Approx. 5'3" – 7'6" to trainer 3'3" – 4'11" to each side (depending on exercise)
- **Framework conditions:** Power connection and Internet connection (Ethernet, Wifi Router)
- **Equipment:** Many exercises can be carried out with equipment (e.g. small dumbbells, medicine balls, gymnastics ball, gymnastics band, stick, etc.). Training without equipment is also possible
- **Functionality:** Every exercise or training program is assigned to a QR code. We provide you with an unlimited number of QR codes to use the station. Every user has the possibility to train at any time with a pre-programmed or personalized training program at a freestanding station

#### ADVANTAGES (expandable/accordion):
- An innovative concept
- Unique selling points to give you an edge in a competitive market
- Can also be integrated into a smaller training area
- The feedback function enables trainees to complete a supervised training session – even without a trainer and 1:1 supervision

### 02 CIRCUIT TRAINING

#### Image:
`https://www.pixformance.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Pixformance-Station-Zirkeltraining-Digitales-Sportgeraet-voll-vernetzt-Personal-Trainer.jpg`

#### H2:
**02 CIRCUIT TRAINING**

#### Body text:
Circuit training for small groups is possible with a minimum of three Pixformance stations.

#### TRAINING CONCEPTS (expandable/accordion):
As 1:1 training in front of the station. Supervised group training is already possible with three of the stations. Prevention courses, according to §20, are possible with four or more stations.

**SINGLE TRAINING INTENSIVE:** The trainee receives tailor-made Pixformance training from the trainer via the online platform. The trainer can track and optimize the training process on the platform at any time.

**GROUP TRAINING:** Supervised group training with up to six people and a trainer is already possible with three Pixformance stations and three intermediate stations (e.g. mat, stepper).

#### DETAILS (expandable/accordion):
- **Required space:** Approx. 65 sq ft, various arrangement options possible depending on the room situation (e.g. row, circle, star)
- **Weight:** 143 lbs
- **Camera:** 2D Full HD
- **Computer and screen:** Can be switched on and off separately
- **Touch-Screen:** 49 inch touch display, standard exercises and training programs directly selectable via touch
- **Training distance:** Approx. 5'3" – 7'6" to trainer 3'3" – 4'11" to each side (depending on exercise)
- **Framework conditions:** Power connection and Internet connection (Ethernet, Wifi Router)
- **Equipment:** Many exercises can be carried out with equipment (e.g. small dumbbells, medicine balls, gymnastics ball, gymnastics band, stick, etc.). Training without equipment is also possible
- **Functionality:** Every exercise or training program is assigned to a QR code. We provide you with an unlimited number of QR codes to use the station. Every user has the possibility to train at any time with a pre-programmed or personalized training program at a freestanding station

#### ADVANTAGES (expandable/accordion):
- An innovative concept
- Unique selling proposition compared to the competitive environment
- Individual support and tailor-made training plans, also possible in groups
- Adaptable to your premises and flexibly expandable
- Interconnected complete solution for training, documentation, and administration
- Prevention courses according to §20 are possible for 4 stations or more
