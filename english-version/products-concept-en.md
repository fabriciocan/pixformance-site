## Section 2: Product Pillar 01 — Pixformance Station

### Number label:
**01**

### H2:
**Pixformance Station**

### Body text:
The patented Pixformance Station is a free-standing digital physical therapy and sports device developed by experts in science, technology, medicine, and fitness. With our innovative software, the station measures the body of each user in a matter of seconds based on 25 joint points. It precisely analyzes the execution of movements and provides live digital feedback — enabling particularly effective and safe rehab and functional training. With more than 350 functional exercises, the station can be used for individual and circuit training.
